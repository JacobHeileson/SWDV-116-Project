About Us

Introduction

Jake’s Outfitters combines the thrill of the outdoors with the comfort of a friendly, home-like environment. We welcome everyone — no matter their background — to experience our ranch to the fullest, in whatever way they choose.

Jake's Story

Jacob (Jake) Heileson grew up fishing with his dad and brother, and was introduced to hunting by a family friend when he was 14.
Although his disability hindered his oppurtunity a little bit, he was ultimately shown that it is possible.
From that moment, he was hooked. He began buying his own gear, and hunting with friends and his brother. But Jake wanted to do things on his own.
He would research day in and day out about hunting different game animals until he knew everything there was to know about them.
He tried baiting for bears, and tried a back-country deer hunt. Though, it was unsucessful, and very taxing on him.
Thats when the dream began: A ranch that gave disabled outdoorsman more oppurtunity.

The Ranch

The ranch sits on 1,500 acres of some of the most beautiful and fruitful land in eastern Idaho.
It’s home to thriving populations of elk, deer, and other native wildlife. We take pride in responsible
land management and ethical hunting practices that keep the ecosystem healthy for generations to come.

Hunters and guests can stay in one of our cozy log cabins or camp anywhere on the property beneath a sky full of stars.
From the first bugle at sunrise to the last crackle of the campfire, every moment here reminds you what the outdoors is truly about.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine what guided hunting means — not just as a sport, but as an experience of freedom, connection, and respect for the land.
We’re committed to providing accessible opportunities, honest guidance, and a community built on integrity. That’s our promise to every guest who steps onto our ranch.
