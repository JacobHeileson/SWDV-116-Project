Jake's Outfitters

How We Started

Jake's Outfitters was started by Jacob Heileson to give disabled and veteran hunters more opportunities.
In a high fence 1,500 acre ranch, owned and maintained by Jacob (and a few helping hands), you the hunter
will have the chance at world class game animals.

Why Choose Jakes Outiftters?

With Jake's Outfitters, you are in control of your experience with us!
With the ability to go solo, or have a guide, an all-inclusive gear bundle, or bring your own,
theres no shortage of options based on your age or experience level.
You get to decide:

  • What you go after
  • How long/when/where youre staying with us (With cozy lodges available, although you can camp anywhere on the property!)
  • What gear you want to bring/have provided
  • How you want your meat butchered processed (DIY or by us)
