Services

At Jake’s Outfitters, we offer a variety of outdoor experiences tailored to your interests, skill level, and sense of adventure.
Whether you're here to hunt, fish, or simply take in the Idaho wilderness, we’re here to help you make the most of your stay.

Hunting

No matter the game youre after, our ranch provides an unforgettable hunting experience across 1,500 acres of diverse terrain. You can choose guided or unguided hunts based on your comfort level:

Guided Hunts

Work with an experienced guide who helps with tracking, field transportation, animal recovery, and optional meat processing.

Unguided Hunts

For independent hunters who want the freedom to explore the land on their own while still benefiting from safe, private property access.

No matter which option you choose, every hunt is designed to respect the land, the wildlife, and ethical hunting practices.

Fishing

Enjoy private access to our creek, which is home to native trout and peaceful scenery perfect for anglers of all ages. You can fish solo or go with a guide who can show you the best spots and techniques for success.
Fishing at Jake’s Outfitters offers a relaxing escape into nature — whether you're fly-fishing at sunrise or casting lines with family.

Camping

Guests are welcome to camp anywhere on the ranch, from shaded wooded areas to open ridgelines with incredible night skies. We offer two options:

Included Camping

Camping is free for all guests who book a hunting or fishing experience.

General Camping

For visitors wishing to come only for the outdoors, camping is available for a small nightly fee. Campers have access to clean water, restrooms, and designated fire pits. Whether you stay in a cozy lodge or sleep under the stars, the property gives you space, quiet, and the freedom to choose your perfect spot.
